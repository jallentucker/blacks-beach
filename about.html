Black's Beach is a rock band based in Portland, Oregon. Lifelong friends Eric Dunn (guitar, vocals) and Colin Speck (guitar, vocals) started the project in their hometown of San Diego, California, before relocating to Portland in 2014. The pair added Casey Davidson (bass) and Josh Tucker (keys/guitar) shortly after arriving in the PNW.
